Introduction

Jade is based on GPT2, and is able to acheive a stunning 88% accuracy in tests against real discord data. Alongside with the new, more powerful model, Jade now comes with more settings than ever before (try typing JD -s or login)

  • Open-Source & Advanced Architecture

    Jade's code is MIT Licenced and publicly available on github, and utilizes modern and advanced algorithms to chat

    Jade's arcitecutre runs on minimal hardware requirments (1vcpu, 1gb of ram) and yet is able to respond withing 0.5 seconds! She is capable of handling multiple loads simultanously.
